An overview of the Enterprise Risk Management Practices at Walmart

In this project we focus on the Health and Wellness division of Walmart.

  1. Governance and culture
    2.

    We start off by creating a team of middle manangers to identify key areas for improvement in risk governance. We suggest strengthening our risk management practices by making our board more diverse and by intorducing a corporate culture program.

  2. Strategic Initiative
    3.

    We introduce our long term strategy for the Health and Wellness division.We discuss the external and internal factors by using PESTEL analysis that could affect our organization and conclude by listing the anticipated changes in the next 5 to 10 years.

  3. Risk Assessment
    4.

    We identified two major risks in regards to our strategy and perform risk assessment in terms of impact, probability, velocity, preparedness. we then conclude by recommending responsing to the current ERM practices.

    1. Cybersecurity risk
    2. Competition Risk

  4. Data Analytics
    5.

    In this section we discuss the external and internal Key Risk Indicators(KRI) we identified for both of our risks.We then showcase our dashboard to be presented to the Board. We also recommended the best Board communication practices.

  5. Key Takeaways
    6.

    In this section we discuss our proposed risk managemnet structure and our choice of Chief Risk Officer(CRO) Candidates
